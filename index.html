It's Not Ours

Historical fiction story by Brayden Lopez

Introduction

It was 1969 when we discovered the truth about our history. Everything we based our ideas on; they aren’t ours. All the major discoveries, all the unbelievable buildings we see are not made by us. Everything on this Earth isn’t meant to be known or discovered. An idea is strong, knowledge is terrifying. This isn’t a conspiracy, this is the truth.

Whatever is out there isn’t trying to hurt us. No. They had the chance thousands of years ago. This probably sounds like a story everybody has heard before. There’s a reason; the truth is coming out. It’s hard to hide a secret in plain sight.
