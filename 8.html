  • CMD Neil Armstrong (Commander)
  • PIL Michael Collins (Command Module Pilot)
  • LMP Edwin "Buzz" E. Aldrin, Jr. (Lunar Module Pilot)
  • POTUS Richard Nixon (President of the United States)
  • DCTR Eugene Francis "Gene" Kranz (Director of NASA)

  • MARINE

    The president is awaiting your presence on the port conference room. Please follow me.

  • POTUS

    Congratulations everyone.

  • CMD

    Thank you Mr President.

  • POTUS

    Let me just say first, how proud I am of all of you. This truly is the most amazing thing humans have ever done.

  • CMD

    Thank you. It is truly an honor.

  • POTUS

    Oh believe me, you and your crew will be getting the rewards you deserve.

  • CMD

    Thank you sir.

  • POTUS

    So what did you find up there?

  • PIL

    You are not going to believe this. We found some sort of ​living​ thing.

  • POTUS

    Aliens?

  • PIL

    I wouldn't dismiss the idea. It would explain some buildings such as Machu Picchu.

  • PIL

    Some say the Incas have descended from the Sun God. Now, I don’t believe in no Sun God, but the fact that the bricks are almost perfectly fitting doesn’t refute the case either. Outside help, while not very scientific, would explain a lot of it.

  • POTUS

    Thats fantastic! But I’m afraid nobody gets to know about this.

  • PIL

    Excuse me?

  • POTUS

    The american people would riot if they got wind of aliens on the moon.

  • PIL

    But sir, the people need to know this.

  • POTUS

    The people need to know that you guys made it to the moon and back. If you utter a word about your rescue mission or any of the “discoveries” you made on the moon, I will personally wipe out all of your work on this mission and kill you and your entire blood line. Are we clear?

  • PIL

    Yes Sir.

  • POTUS

    And the rest of you?

  • CMD

    Yes Sir.

  • LMP

    Yes Sir.

  • POTUS

    And you? Director.

  • DCTR

    Yes Sir...

  • POTUS

    Good. You are all dismissed.

  • POTUS

    I've got it handled from here.
