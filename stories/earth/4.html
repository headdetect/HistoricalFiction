War Room

  • DCTR Eugene Francis "Gene" Kranz (Director of NASA)
  • CAPCOM ​McCandless (Capsule Communicator)
  • ACAP Assistant CAPCOM
  • POTUS Richard Nixon (President of the United States)

  • DCTR

    We called you here Mr. President in regards to the Apollo 11 mission.

  • POTUS

    Continue.

  • DCTR

    About 20 minutes after our men landed in the Sea of Tranquility we lost their signal. This was after we heard of possible quakes. Our men are still alive up there and we cannot afford to lose them. We have a war vessel that we were building for the Department of Defence for surveillance of the Soviets that we could possibly launch to rescue them. It would only be able to stay out of orbit for no longer than 2 days.

  • POTUS

    How much is this going to cost us?

  • DCTR

    Aside from the 480 billion we’ve already spent? I would estimate it to be another 60 billion We already have the ship built just needs to be fired up. After we prep it of course.

  • POTUS

    Are you out of your mind. You would make Kennedy proud, but the people already don’t like that we are spending billions of their tax dollars on your little experiment. And I’ll...

  • CAPCOM

    EXPERIMENT!? This is the future! This is going to carve a path for the US to be the best in everything. This is how we beat the god damn Soviets! If we let the damn Commies have the first successful manned mission to the moon, YOU are going to have some serious trouble on your hands.

  • POTUS

    First off. Don’t you ever cut me off again. Secondly. Those 3 men aren’t worth 60 billion. Thirdly, I’ve already made my mind up. Start packing up boys.

  • CAPCOM

    Do you want another mission failure on your hands?

  • POTUS

    I don’t give a damn.

  • DCTR

    Sir. Please. We beg of you. We need these men back on Earth.

  • POTUS

    This meeting is over. Now, if you'd excuse me, I have a date with Pat. I’m not going to miss it over this.

  • POTUS Leaves room

  • CAPCOM

    Sir, you can’t honestly do nothing.

  • DCTR

    Don’t worry. Something will be done.

  • DCTR

    How long would it take you to get the prototype prepped and ready for launch.

  • CAPCOM

    I estimate an hour or so. We just need to boot up the satellite processes and spin up some instances of the piloting nodes.

  • DCTR

    ​ I want it prepped and ready to go an hour ago.

  • CAPCOM

    Yes sir.
